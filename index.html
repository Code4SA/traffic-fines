Fine revenues per municipality

Are fines being used to save lives or earn revenue? Code for South Africa is investigating what we all suspect. Using quarterly financial reports we have plotted revenue from fines earned since July 2011. Municipalities have budgeted R1.7 billion in revenues for the 2014 financial year.

Surprisingly, some municipalities seem to earn a large percentage of their revenue from fines. Also, the Western Cape and Northern Cape earn relatively more money from fines than other provinces.

We're also exploring the question of whether those municipalities which the N1 and N2 go through earn relatively more from fines than others. Initial results seem to confirm this.
